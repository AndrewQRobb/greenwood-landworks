About Us

Heavy Iron.
Local Roots.

Greenwood Landscaping was built on a simple idea: bring the right equipment and the right operator to every job. Based in Charlottesville, Virginia, we serve homeowners, farmers, contractors, and developers across Albemarle County and the surrounding Blue Ridge foothills.


We're not a franchise. We're not a crew with a truck and a chainsaw. We run heavy iron — excavators, skid steers, dozers, and commercial mowing rigs — because that's what it takes to do the job right, on time, and without cutting corners.

Reliability

We show up when we say we will, with the right equipment for the job. No excuses, no delays, no half-measures. Our reputation is built one completed project at a time.

Local Knowledge

Virginia soil, Virginia grades, Virginia weather — we know the land because we live on it. From the red clay of Albemarle to the rocky slopes of the Blue Ridge, we understand what the terrain demands.

Right Equipment

Every job gets the right machine. We don't show up with undersized equipment and hope for the best. Our fleet is built to handle real work — efficiently, safely, and on schedule.
