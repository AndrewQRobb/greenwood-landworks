Underground Utilities — Virginia 811

It is the customer's responsibility to call Virginia 811 (Miss Utility) at least 3 business days before the scheduled dig date. You can reach them at 811 or 1-800-552-7001, or submit a request at virginia811.com.

Greenwood Land Works is not responsible for damage to underground utilities (water, sewer, gas, electric, cable, fiber, etc.) that were not properly marked by Miss Utility prior to the start of work. If you are unsure whether you need to call 811, call anyway — it's free.
