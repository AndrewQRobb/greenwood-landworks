Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Greenwood Land Works LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website greenwoodlandworks.com. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding that information.

Information We Collect

When you fill out our contact or quote request form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Type of service you're interested in
  • A description of your project

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide through our contact form. We do not collect information from other sources.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Schedule site visits and estimates
  • Follow up on quotes and project details
  • Communicate about ongoing or upcoming work

Third-Party Form Processing

Our contact form is processed through Formspree, a third-party form handling service. When you submit our form, your information passes through Formspree's servers before reaching us. Formspree has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data in transit. We chose Formspree because it does not use your data for marketing or sell it to third parties.

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is used solely for the business purposes described above.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses privacy-friendly analytics (Cloudflare Web Analytics or Vercel Analytics) to understand general traffic patterns, such as which pages are visited most often. These tools do not use tracking cookies, do not collect personal information, and do not follow you across other websites.

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies of any kind.

Data Retention

We keep inquiry data for as long as it is useful for business purposes, such as following up on estimates, scheduling work, or maintaining records of past projects. If you'd like us to delete your information, just ask.

Your Rights

You can contact us at any time to:

  • Ask what information we have about you
  • Request that we update or correct your information
  • Request that we delete your information

Changes to This Policy
