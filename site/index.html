What We Do

Our Services

From raw land to finished grade — we bring the right equipment and a light touch to get it done right, with minimal disturbance to the surrounding landscape.

01 — Clearing

Land & Brush Clearing

We take a selective approach to clearing — removing what needs to go while preserving mature trees, natural drainage, and the character of your land. Whether you're prepping a building site or restoring overgrown pasture, we clear with purpose, not a scorched-earth approach.

02 — Excavation

Earth Moving & Excavation

From foundation digs to pond excavation, we match the right equipment to the scale of the job. That means using compact machines where they'll do, and bringing in heavier iron only when the work demands it — keeping soil compaction and site disruption to a minimum.

03 — Grading

Grading & Site Preparation

Good grading works with the natural lay of the land, not against it. We grade for new construction, drainage correction, and pad preparation while respecting existing water flow patterns and minimizing runoff into neighboring properties and waterways.

04 — Walls

Retaining Walls

Engineered retaining walls that manage slopes, prevent erosion, and create usable space on Virginia's rolling terrain. We build with block, boulder, and timber — choosing materials that fit the site and complement the natural landscape.

05 — Driveways

Driveways & Access Roads

We build driveways and access roads that follow the natural contour of your property — clearing, grading, base prep, and surface. Proper drainage design means less erosion and a road that lasts through Virginia's seasons without washing out.

06 — Mowing

Bushhogging & Mowing

Keep large acreage under control without overworking the land. Our commercial mowing equipment handles fields, fence lines, and overgrown lots that standard mowers can't touch — maintaining your property's natural balance.

About Us

Small Footprint.
Big Results.

Greenwood Land Works was built on a simple idea: do the job right while treating the land with respect. Based in Charlottesville, Virginia, we serve homeowners, farmers, contractors, and developers across Albemarle, Greene, and Nelson counties.


We believe that heavy equipment work doesn't have to mean heavy impact. Every project starts with understanding the land — its drainage, its soil, its mature trees, and its natural features. We use the right-sized equipment for each job, keep our working footprint as small as possible, and leave your property better than we found it.


We're not a franchise. We're a locally owned operation that takes pride in environmentally sensitive land management across the Blue Ridge foothills.

01

Minimum Impact

We use the smallest equipment that will get the job done. Less soil compaction, less disruption to drainage patterns, less damage to surrounding vegetation. The goal is to change what needs changing and leave everything else untouched.

02

Local Knowledge

Virginia soil, Virginia grades, Virginia weather — we know the land because we live on it. From the red clay of Albemarle to the rocky mountain slopes of Greene and Nelson, we understand what the terrain demands.

03

Environmentally Mindful

We protect existing waterways, preserve mature trees when possible, manage erosion through proper grading and drainage, and follow best practices for sediment control. Your land is an asset — we treat it that way.
