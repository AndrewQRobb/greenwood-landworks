About Us

Small Footprint.
Big Results.

Greenwood Land Works was built on a simple idea: do the job right while treating the land with respect. Based in Charlottesville, Virginia, we serve homeowners, farmers, contractors, and developers across Albemarle, Greene, and Nelson counties.

We believe that heavy equipment work doesn't have to mean heavy impact. Every project starts with understanding the land — its drainage, its soil, its mature trees, and its natural features. We use the right-sized equipment for each job, keep our working footprint as small as possible, and leave your property better than we found it.

We're not a franchise. We're a locally owned operation that takes pride in environmentally sensitive land management across the Blue Ridge foothills.

Minimum Impact

We use the smallest equipment that will get the job done. Less soil compaction, less disruption to drainage, less damage to surrounding vegetation.

Local Knowledge

Virginia soil, Virginia grades, Virginia weather. From the red clay of Albemarle to the rocky mountain slopes of Greene and Nelson, we know what the terrain demands.

Environmentally Mindful

We protect existing waterways, preserve mature trees when possible, manage erosion through proper grading, and follow best practices for sediment control.

Right-Sized for the Job

We maintain a range of equipment so we can match the machine to the work — not the other way around.
